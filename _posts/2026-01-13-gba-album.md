Are you a Music-Spreadsheet Wizard?

Do you like your `mod`s and `it`s more than your `wav`s and `mp3`s?

Are you a fan of the limit, as opposed to the limitless?

Today I am introducing a project I've had on my mind for almost a year called TrackPak. TrackPak is a tool that converts your tracker music to an album that runs entirely on the Game Boy Advance.

TrackPak supports several customization options such as fonts, color schemes, and additional details about you and your music.
